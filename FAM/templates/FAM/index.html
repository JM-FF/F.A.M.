Meet Our FAM

FAM is a financial asset management system that aims to automate cryptocurrency portfolio performance tracking. Create your secure account, effortlessly manage your portfolio, and track cross-exchange investments. Join the FAM today.

API

With FAM, APIs are responsible for pulling real time market data and user investments to make sure investors are getting time accurate cryptocurrency prices. FAM simplifies investment tracking by automatically pulling in investors trades from their exchange account.
