Meet Our FAM

FAM challenges the complicated status quo of investment tracking by providing users with the ability to track their assets in one place. Create your secure account, effortlessly manage your portfolio, and track cross-market investments; Join the FAM today.

Cross-Market Investment Tracking

With FAM, anyone can create a portfolio that tracks the most popular assets, stocks, options, and cryptocurrencies, in one place. Live data is used to accurately and efficiently monitor the performance of investments over-time.

Security

We value keeping your investment information private as much as you do. F.A.M ensures the security of users’ investment information by using the django authentication system. Your assets will be accessed and managed by you and secured by us.

Flexibility

F.A.M. allows users to effortlessly manage and track their assets by providing an up to date balance and overview of their entire portfolio. Users can add and delete assets to see how they affect their overall performance without the fear of altering their actual holdings. The flexibility of F.A.M. provides a platform to test and analyze potential future investments.
